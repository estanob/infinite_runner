Surf Bending

Welcome!

This is an Avatar the Last Airbender themed 2-D infinite surfing game.

Instructions

Don't touch the icebergs! Use water bending powers to push them out of the way.
Move around with the arrow keys
Press space to water bend!.

Remember!

For a good water bender, it takes time to create a powerful wave.
Your waves will not be strong enough right away to take out the icebergs.
